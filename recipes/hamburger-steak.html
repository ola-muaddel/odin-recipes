Hamburger Steak

These juicy Hamburger Steaks are a family favorite! Made with simple pantry ingredients, this quick and easy recipe delivers big flavor and is perfect for busy weeknights when you need a hearty, home-cooked meal fast.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 egg
  • ½ cup breadcrumbs
  • ¼ cup milk
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil (for frying)

For the sauce :

  • ½ cup beef broth
  • 2 tbsp ketchup
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp cornstarch (optional, for thickening)

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix ground beef, chopped onion, egg, breadcrumbs, milk, salt, and pepper.
  2. Shape the mixture into patties.
  3. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat and cook patties 4–5 minutes per side until browned and cooked through.
  4. Remove patties from the pan. In the same pan, mix beef broth, ketchup, Worcestershire sauce, and soy sauce. Cook until slightly thickened.
  5. Pour the sauce over the patties and serve hot.
